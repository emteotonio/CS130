Model Mistreatment

The entertainment industry has a long history of exploiting entertainers to turn over a profit. This stretches from discrimination to overworking and extending even to blackmail or sexual abuse.

The modeling industry is no better, known for perpetuating unrealistic standards and mistreatment of models.

Though many have spoken of their experiences, these are the stories of four women that can bring light to the topic.

  1. Caroline Cossey is a transgender model from the '80s who outed, ruining her career.
  2. Tess Holliday is a current plus-sized professional model who faces harrassment and discrimination due to her size.
  3. Mia Khalifa is an ex-porn star and webcam model who was exploited and faced difficulty with harrassment and employment.
  4. Emily Ratajkowski is a runway and fashion model who faced sexual harrasment and abuse.
